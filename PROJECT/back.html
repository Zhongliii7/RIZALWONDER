1. in 1896 Jose Rizal wrote the Saint Eustaquio Martyr in Calamba


  2. Father Sanchez, his favorite teacher, ask him to write a drama based on the prose story of St. Eustace the Martyr.


  3. He wrote the religious drama in poetic verses. June 02, 1876 - finished the manuscript


  4. He submitted the finished manuscript entitled “San Eustacio, Martir” (St. Eustace, the Martyr) to Father Sanchez in his last academic year in Ateneo
