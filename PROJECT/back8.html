  2. Mi ultimo adios (My Last Farewell) was Jose Rizal's final testament of his love for a country he felt was being trodden underfoot by intruders from abroad.


  3. The piece was one of the last notes he wrote before his death


  4. Mi Ultimo Adios is a poem that tells us how beautiful the Philippines is and how Rizal wants us to learn from our past and to see the truth about how the world view us.
